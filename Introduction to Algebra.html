What is Algebra?

Algebra uses symbols (like x or y) to represent numbers in formulas and equations. It's a fundamental part of mathematics used to find unknown values.

Basic Concepts

  • Variables: Letters that represent numbers (e.g., x, y)
  • Expressions: Combinations of variables, numbers, and operations (e.g., 2x + 3)
  • Equations: Statements that two expressions are equal (e.g., 2x + 3 = 7)
